‚ö° How It Works

1. Provide your video file (MP4 or similar).

Simply select your video and thumbnail via the intuitive desktop app interface.

2. AI generates title, description, and hashtags automatically.

Leveraging Whisper for transcription and Llama 3.2 for intelligent metadata creation.

3. Authenticates securely with your YouTube account.

Seamless OAuth integration ‚Äî secure, one-time setup without manual API handling.

4. Uploads video automatically, showing progress and final video ID.

Real-time progress tracking with instant confirmation and thumbnail application.
